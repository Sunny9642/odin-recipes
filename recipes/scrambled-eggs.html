Scrambled eggs

Description

Scrambled Eggs that you make in a pan

Ingredients

  • 2 eggs
  • cooking oil
  • salt shaker
  • pepper shaker

Steps

  1. Pour oil into your pan, and turn on the heat
  2. Crack two eggs into the pan
  3. Stir the eggs around, while adding salt and pepper
  4. When the eggs look how you want them, they're done!
